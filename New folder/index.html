WELCOME TO RAJSHAHI MOHILA POLYTECHNIC

Rajshahi mohila Polytechnic Institute is a historical polytechnic institute. This institute is playing a vital role in Technical sector of Bangladesh which runs under Bangladesh Technical Education Board. Its history begins in 1924 when the Landlord of Trash Mr. Shri Banomali Roy founded a technical school at the right side of the Nagarabari highway in Rajshahi mohila named “BL Eliot Technical School”. In the decade of 1960’s, Government of East Pakistan established many Polytechnic Institute in different district of East Pakistan under Five-Year Plan .
